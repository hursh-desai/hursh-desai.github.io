Profit Optimization Under Uncertainty

In this project I used Crystal Ball to simulate the profit distribution of a particular airline flight while minimizing costs. I had to use simulation modeling because demand was uncertain. I used a uniform distribution and a given mean and st. dev. to model the demand; and I used a binomial distribution to model how many passangers who bought a ticket actually showed up. This airline could overbook for both coach and business class so I had to optimize the profit distribution through the # of tickets sold given that there was a cost for bumping a passanger.
